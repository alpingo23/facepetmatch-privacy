Privacy Policy - Face Pet Match

This Privacy Policy explains how the Face Pet Match application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") processes user data.

1. Data Collected

The Application does not collect users' photos or personal data. Photos are analyzed solely using the face-api.js library on our servers, and this process occurs entirely on the servers. No data is sent to or stored on local devices.

2. Data Usage

The analyzed data is processed on servers exclusively for the pet matching feature and is not used for any purpose outside the Application.

3. Data Sharing

The Application does not share any user data with third parties. Photos or analysis results are not exported externally.

4. Policy Regarding Children

This Application is not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13, as no data collection occurs on local devices.

5. User Rights

Since the Application does not collect data on local devices, users do not need to request data deletion or access. For any questions, you can contact us at alpipekciler@gmail.com.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated as needed. Changes will be announced within the Application.
